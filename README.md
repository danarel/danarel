## About Me

I'm a communications and leadership professional passionate about building thriving teams and empowering people to grow in their work and leadership journey. As a department director and professional coach, I help individuals and organizations develop strategies that align with their values and strengthen collective leadership.

My approach blends practical communication systems with a deeper understanding of human-centered design, organizational psychology, and leadership theory — all aimed at creating more equitable, sustainable workplaces.

Outside of my role, I love exploring how technology, coaching, and storytelling intersect to enhance leadership development and organizational effectiveness.

## What I'm Working On
